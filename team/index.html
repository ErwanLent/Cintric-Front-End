Meet The Team

Chief Executive Officer

Connor Bowlan

Connor studied sociology at the University of California, Merced. He previously served as the technical cofounder of a startup that built tools to automate bookings for residential cleaning services. Connor has spent time all over the world, most recently in Botswana where he attended the University of Botswana, studied micro-social interactions, and explored Southern Africa by motorcycle.

Chief Technology Officer

Erwan Lent

Erwan is a full stack developer with an insatiable urge to apply technology where it's needed the most. Erwan created his first company at 17, focused on improving school security and communication. At 18, he dropped out of college and moved on to UC Santa Barbara where he built web applications for the Office of Financial Aid with a talented team of developers. Erwan now has grown his roots into the startup world.

Chief Product Officer

Joel Green

Joel is a mobile and python developer. Over the last several years, Joel has spent time developing robotics, conducting undergraduate research at UCSB, building automated lab tools, and participating in hackathons as an iOS developer. Previous to engineering, Joel has spent time as a Jazz pianist and music composer.

Chief Operating Officer

Rhett Rainen

Rhett graduated cum laude from UC Merced with a BA in Economics/ BS in Management. During his university years he used creative financing to start a flourishing portfolio of rental properties. After graduation he promptly left the country to ride a motorcycle through Vietnam, and backpack through Asia/Europe. He has spent the last year as a business administrator at Nasa's Jet Propulsion Laboratory.
