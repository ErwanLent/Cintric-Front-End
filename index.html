What Is Cintric?

Cintric is a universal platform that allows businesses to contact consumers within their location. Simply set the location and boundary for your business, then send a message to consumers through our responsive web interface. Cintric even provides rich analytics on who is in your location. It’s that simple, and can be set up in minutes.
