What Is Cintric?

Cintric is a universal platform that allows businesses to contact consumers within their location. Simply set the location and boundary for your business, then send a message to consumers through our responsive web interface. Cintric even provides rich analytics on who is in your location. It’s that simple, and can be set up in minutes.

Practical Applications

  • Coffee Shop

    With Cintric, you can notify your customers of whatever you wish. Even the smallest details like a fresh batch of pastries can be what brings a customer further into the story you tell with your business.

  • Concert

    Keep attendees informed of your story’s developments by letting them know they’re a part of it and in the loop. Cintric only notifies people in your location, so you can communicate details to the people who care without bothering the people who don’t.

  • Emergency

    Stories can happen in the present tense, and so can Cintric. Notify users in the area of what they need to know now. Broadcast via push notifications that will pop up on user’s lock screens.
